User Research

User research: summary

Throughout the project, multiple activities were planned and conducted starting with identifying the problem, discussing the same, identifying the user base and creating empathy maps for the same, and again discussing the goals and objectives discovered.
I also designed and conducted interviews in addition to usability study as well as competitive audit and came to numerous conclusions listing them all in the form of affinity diagrams, hence identifying priorities and insights and finally including all necessary details to form mockups and prototypes with planned presentations and interviews to check and revise the same.

User research: pain points

Meal tracker: To track calories throughout the day and be mindful of what one eats

Recipes: Recipes that suit the user's taste and be mindful of the diet

Restaurants: Nearby places that cater to the diets and adhere to user's needs

Exercise: For a healthy body and mind exercise is essential with the right nutrition

Persona: Carina

Problem statement:
Carina is a working mother who requires a nutrition app that not only tracks her daily nutrition but also reminds her of the same, have accessible restaurants that serve meals aligning with her goal and easy to cook recipes since she doesn't have enough time to prepare meals for all the members of the family and manage work

Persona: Karan

Problem statement:
Karan is a freelance videographer who happens to travel a lot and has an erratic lifestyle hence would like an app that fits well with his lifestyle and helps him improve the same with respect to nutrition, exercise, hydration, reminders, and sleep schedule.

Accessibility Considerations

1. Accessible icons and alt text is provided plus major options such as map and profile are provided on the screen in addition to the icons in the bottom bar

2. The icons used are clear and easy to understand

3. Help option is provided on each page for any issues the user might face
